03The play

Three moves separate durable advantage from one-off pilots.

Pick all three, in this order.

Move 01

Anchor on a workflow, not a model.

Pick the highest-friction patient or clinician workflow. Build around it. Models change every six months. Workflows do not.

Move 02

Ship a working prototype in 30 days.

Demos beat decks. A prototype with real data and a real user in the loop earns the next conversation. A PowerPoint earns a meeting.

Move 03

Design for the audit, not just the user.

FDA, EMA, internal compliance. Build traceability into the experience from day one. Retrofitting governance is where pilots die.

04The bet

We win this by treating AI as infrastructure, not a feature.

The teams who treat AI as a deliverable will keep shipping POCs. The teams who treat it as the substrate underneath every workflow will own the next decade of care delivery. Lilly should be in that second camp by Q4.
